Phil Hardberger Park FAQ:

Are bikes allowed?
The trails are multi-use, which means walkers, runners, bicycles, strollers, roller-blades and skateboards are permitted on the trails.
Are we allowed to go off-trail?
Please stay on the trails. Hiking off-trail is prohibited to protect the native habitat.
Can we camp?
Camping is not allowed at Phil Hardberger Park.
Are dogs allowed?
Dogs are allowed but, must be on a 6-foot leash when not inside the dog parks.
Can we feed the wild life?
The feeding or touching of any wild animals in the park is prohibited.
Are there BBQ pits?
Barbecue pits are available in picnic areas where grilling is permitted. Personal barbecue pits and grills are not allowed in the park
Are fireworks allowed?
Fireworks and smoke bombs are prohibited in the park.
Are we under a Burn-Ban?
Yes, we are currently under a Burn-Ban.
No, we are not currently under a burn-ban.
Is tobacco permitted?
Smoking and all forms of tobacco use is prohibited.
Is alcohol permitted?
Alcohol is prohibited in the park.
